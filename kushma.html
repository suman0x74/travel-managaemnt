Kushma Tour

Situated in the western part of Nepal Kushma is a beautiful hilly municipality filled with picturesque villages, hills, rivers, and sceneries. Cable car, Saligram sila, longest suspension bridge, religious destination etc. are some of the famous tourist attraction places in Kushma. Besides exploring and experiencing the natural beauty of this city, it also carries historical significance which is home to many spiritual places like Hampal, Kalanjar, Deurali etc. Also, it is a route for Khopra trekking.
Price: It costs NPR 1500 per person for Nepali nationality, NPR.4000 per person for SAARC + CHINESE nationality, and USD 50 for other nationality. The rate is inclusive of lunch, transportation, rafting and tax.
