Mustang Tour

The trek into the kingdom of Mustang is an unforgettable experience, lying behind the main mountain in the rain shadow area.Tour to Upper Mustang is the experience of Trans Himalaya of Nepal bordering Tibet. Explore panoramic mountain views surroundings and scenic Plateau landscape. Upper Mustang also called mini Tibet as it is the same landscape, peoples and their cultures are the same as Tibetan. Mustang Tour Cost for Nepali is depending on how you want to make it. We can organize it as per your interest, timeframe and your budget.
Price: It costs NPR 6500 per person for Nepali nationality, NPR.14000 per person for SAARC + CHINESE nationality, and USD 450 for other nationality. The rate is inclusive of lunch, transportation, rafting and tax.
